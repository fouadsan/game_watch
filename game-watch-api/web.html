Games Tracker App

Games Tracker is an app where you keep track of all the games you are interested in by tracking them, you can mark your playing status on tracked games.

It allows you to check upcoming, most popular games easily filtered by the platforms of your choice, it recommends you games based on the games you have tracked and you can optionally get notified about the newly released games from your tracked collection or selected platforms.

Whats different?: you can track a series of games, see your playing progress on it and get info / notifications on upcoming or newly released games in that series.
